About CV. Irsal Funiture

Founded in 1998, CV. AGUNG Furniture has been at the forefront of furniture design and manufacturing, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern 3D design technology.

Our team of skilled artisans and designers work together to create unique pieces that reflect both functionality and aesthetic appeal. We source sustainable materials and prioritize eco-friendly production methods.

Sustainable Materials

We use eco-friendly, responsibly sourced wood and materials.

Custom Designs

Premium Quality

Nationwide Delivery

We deliver and install your furniture anywhere in the country.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive furniture solutions from design to installation.

3D Design & Planning

Custom Manufacturing

Professional Installation
